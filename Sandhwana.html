Chapter: Sandwana

Captured during the vibrant cultural fest RAGAM 2025 at NIT Calicut, this photo series celebrates the classical elegance of Sandhwana through expressive dance. Shot using a Canon 200D Mark II with both a 50mm f/1.8 prime and a 75–250mm telephoto lens, the frames capture intricate hand gestures, graceful movements, and emotive expressions — all frozen against the warm stage lights. Each image echoes rhythm, tradition, and grace, blending candid moments with the timeless essence of Bharatanatyam. This chapter is a tribute to the harmony between art and motion, spotlighting Sandhwana’s storytelling through dance.
