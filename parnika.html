Chapter: Parnika Srija

This photo series celebrates Parnika Srija in a radiant yellow outfit that effortlessly steals the spotlight. Captured at Kozhikode Beach, the visuals reflect her vibrant spirit and natural grace — from candid beach strolls to animated street scenes, and even beneath the charm of a Ferris wheel. Each frame echoes her charisma and the cultural warmth she brings to the lens. Shot on a Canon 200D Mark II using a 75–250mm telephoto lens and a 50mm f/1.8 prime lens to blend intimacy with depth and distance.
