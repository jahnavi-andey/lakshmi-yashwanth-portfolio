Chapter: Princy Khatri

This photo series captures the timeless elegance of Princy Khatri as she beautifully represents the cultural richness of Jammu and Kashmir. Draped in a traditional ensemble adorned with a delicate veil and ornate headpiece, she brings poise and heritage into every frame — from serene landscapes to bustling urban scenes. Shot on a Canon 200D Mark II using a 50mm f/1.8 prime lens and a 75–250mm telephoto lens, each shot balances intimacy and grandeur with warm, golden tones.
